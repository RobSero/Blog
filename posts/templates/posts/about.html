About the Blog

What I lack in Blog length, I make up for in enthusiasm and sweet HTML effects

Heya. My name is Rob and I am just a guy with a passion for building things but doing it in the most sustainable way possible. I have worked on over 100 residential architecture projects and currently building apps to support people and businesses in their daily lives.

It’s cool to be clean now - Nobody wants to live a wasteful life, but (understandably) nobody wants to splash the cash - But it doesn’t have to be a choice between one or the other! It’s not a secret that were probably doing more damage to our planet than we should be (not to mention all the stuff we are not told about…) but after working with a lot of people on their dream homes, there is definitely this mindset that to go sustainable, it is going to cost you a lot. I'm just learning about stuff, then writing it to solidify my thoughts. If it helps you then cool, if not then you don't have to keep reading :)
